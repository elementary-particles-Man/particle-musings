THP Part 2: Strategy and the System of Seven Documents

The Horizon Protocol (also known as: PCAF / PG / KF / Ark-R)

1. Name and Multilayered Identity

This project does not rely on a single name, but has multiple faces depending on the situation.
The Horizon Protocol (THP) is the flagship name and the central designation used in internal documents and official records.
Academically, it is positioned as the Pax Communis – Accordia Framework (PCAF) and is responsible for the ideological and theoretical framework.
Project Genesis (PG) is used for general public relations to intuitively convey the "genesis of a new world" to people.
The Kyrios Framework (KF) is used for institutional design and financial protocols to provide stability to regulations and international agreements.
Also, from a historical perspective, the name Ark-R remains, indicating continuity from past projects.

2. Basic Concept

The foundation of THP is "transparency, fairness, and non-hegemony." It is an implementation structure for universally protecting life and order, abandoning the narrative of hegemony and superiority.
It is not just an economic bailout, but a redesign of the entire order, including humanity, ethnicity, religion, and ethics.
It is not just a philosophy, but is practically defined by seven basic documents, bridging the three layers of institutions, norms, and operations.

3. Overview of the Seven Basic Documents

① World Redefinition Charter

This document buries the old narrative of the world order and declares the starting point of a new story.
It universalizes the standard that "those who break the order are guilty" and institutionalizes atonement and condemnation. It sets the three pillars of transparency, fairness, and accuracy as its banner.

② Ethics-Charter

It defines universal human sin and atonement, and presents a new framework for the judiciary, military, and police to ensure order.
It specifies practical institutional designs such as the Hague Ethical Court (Court of Human Ethics), the International Police (ICPO-2), and the War Prevention Force (UN-PDF).

③ People-Charter

All peoples are horizontal and are beings to be respected by each other.
It codifies the principles of freedom of peoples, respect for culture, denial of superiority, and coexistence, and denies exclusion and discrimination as destruction of order.

④ Religion-Charter

Based on the major premise that "your religion is also right, and my religion is also right," it balances freedom of belief and respect for science.
It defines religion as a path to save people and denies cults and the use of violence.

⑤ Lexicon

This is a basic dictionary that organizes the concepts and technical terms used in THP.
It provides a common language for understanding the entire project, including THP, Walpurgis, Fiat-Rev2, Peace Utility Layer, and E-MAD.

⑥ Ops-KPI-Dashboard

A "health checkup" that diagnoses the state of the world with numbers.
It constantly monitors US Treasury auction bid-to-cover ratios, the dollar index, Chinese LGFV debt, European government bond spreads, etc., and enables automatic intervention (swaps, redundancy) in advance of a crisis.

⑦ Appendix (LSC)

This is an ideological addendum to LSC (Libra/Scales Capitalism), and its principle is to "start from reality and improve reality" through seven core propositions.
It is a thinking OS that emphasizes improvement rather than innovation and supports order while constantly changing its form.

4. Conclusion

The seven documents are not just a collection of ideas, but a feasible manual for maintaining order in a crisis.
They are independent yet complementary, and are a guide to lead all of humanity to a "future where no one is left behind."
Through its multilayered identity and the seven documents, THP institutionalizes the trinity of market, life, and humanity, and becomes a practical prescription for overcoming a historic turning point.
